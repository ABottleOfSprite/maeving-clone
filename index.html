CHARGING MADE EASY

The entire design of a Maeving revolves around the concept of removable batteries.

To charge your bike, all you need to do is remove the battery and charge it from any standard socket, just like your smartphone, or plug it into the mains and charge onboard.

This means you never need to worry about planning where to charge, because you can literally ‘refuel’ wherever there is power.

To charge the RM1S from 20-80% takes under 3 hours and 0-100% in 6 hours.

Say goodbye to range anxiety.
